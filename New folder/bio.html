Hey There! I'm Fafa

PEACE FAFA POMANEY was born on October 15 in Agbozume in the Volta Region of Ghana where she attained her primary,Junior High and Senoir High Certificate. She started school at the age of 5 and she is the First and Only Child of her parents.

In septemberr 2014, she moved to Accra to continue her education. She started her tertiary education in Octorber 2014 at the Ghana Institute of Journalism where she graduated with a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Communication Studies in August 2018. She started working even before completing her tertiary education. She is currently doing her National Service at iSpace Foundation as the media content creator. She loves her job.

Peace Fafa Pomaney is a libra since she was born. She is a calm, reserved person who is not easily angered. She is caring and loves sharing with others. She hates dishonesty, cruelty and disloyalty. Fafa grew up in an environment where she was not allowed to mingle with people thus making her an introvert. She likes food a lot. Music heals her when she is hurting. She likes watching movies amd would love to act in them in the future.

Fafa as she prefers to be called, has dreams of becoming one of the best media moguls in the world. She hopes to become an actor, a script writer, a model, a philantropist and a lawyer!
